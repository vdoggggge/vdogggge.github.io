Card Game Tabletop Cold War

Iron Curtain

A competitive two-player card game set during the Cold War era.

Role: Game Designer & System Designer
Click to see more ↓

About the Game

A two-player competitive card game inspired by the Cold War conflict between two nations. Each round, players choose weapon and economy cards to outplay their opponent. The game rewards both strategic planning and reading your opponent's hand.

Core Mechanic

Players manage two types of cards — green economy cards and red weapon cards, each with three power levels. Cards are placed simultaneously each round, so you have to predict what the other player will do. Blank "permanent" cards add a bluffing layer to every turn.

What I Did

Designed the full card system, game board, and round structure. Created all card illustrations and the box art. Playtested extensively to balance the weapon and economy cards. Inspired by Sheriff of Nottingham's hidden information mechanics.

Stealth Puzzle Game Jam Ludum Dare 54

Silent Echoes

A top-down stealth puzzle game built for Ludum Dare 54 — "Limited Space."

Role: Game Designer & Asset Creator
Click to see more ↓

About the Game

A top-down stealth puzzle game where you play as a blind protagonist navigating a limited space. You rely on restricted senses and limited abilities to survive and find your way through each level. Built in 72 hours for the global Ludum Dare 54 game jam.

Core Mechanic

The player can't see the full map — vision is intentionally limited to sell the "blind protagonist" concept. Movement and interaction are carefully constrained to match the jam theme of "Limited Space." Every decision feels tense because of what you can't see.

What I Did

Designed the game concept and levels. Created and animated all character and environment assets. This was my first game jam — proud we finished a complete, playable game within the 72-hour window.
